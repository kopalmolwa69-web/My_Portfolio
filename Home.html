AI & Data Science

“AI and Data Science together help machines learn from data and make intelligent decisions.”
“Data Science and AI work together. Data Science collects and analyzes data, and AI uses that data to make smart decisions.”

➤ They create systems that can learn from data and make decisions automatically.
📊 Data Science → works on data (collect, analyze, understand).
🤖 Artificial Intelligence → uses that data to act smart.




About AI

"AI(Artificial Intelligence) means giving machines the ability to think, learn, and make decisions like humans." AI is the ability of a computer or machine to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.
💡 Easy Explanation
➤ Humans can learn, think, and decide.
➤ AI tries to make machines do the same.
👉 So, AI = “Smart machines”






About Data Science

"Data Science is the process of collecting, analyzing, and understanding data to get useful information and make decisions." Data Science is a field that uses data to find patterns, gain insights, and help in decision-making.
💡 Easy Explanation
➤ Data = information (numbers, text, etc.)
➤ Data Science = working on that data to understand it
👉 So, Data Science = “Making sense of data”
